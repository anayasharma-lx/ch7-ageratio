Age Ratio — Adding Shifts It

When Neelima was 3 her mother was 30 — ratio 1:10. Nine years later: 12 and 39 — ratio 4:13. The age gap stays constant, but the ratio changes every year. Adding the same number to both terms of a ratio is different from multiplying — only multiplication preserves proportionality. Drag the timeline to watch the ratio drift.
